\section{Conclusion}

Kungfu is a product thesis about the next layer of agent work. As agents become
more capable, the limiting problem shifts from single-turn intelligence to
durable coordination in the real world. People and agents need facts that do
not drift, trust that starts from those facts, cooperation that begins from
trusted value, and timelines that declare their observer.

The immediate route is practical: build a local-first product that manages real
work with agents, dogfood it against private real-world work, verify its public
surfaces through KFD and Buildchain, and let extensions grow from a stable
core.

If the product succeeds, Kungfu is not merely a tool for debugging agents. It
becomes a runtime fact layer and control plane for real-world work with agents.
